<br> #### Week 1: Design and Cutting This is my initial Fusion360 design of my press fit model. The circle has a diameter of 40mm, with each notch having the width of 4.1mm. The cardboard material is around 4.2mm thick, so 0.1mm is an estimation of how much kerf there will be. <img src="press.png" alt="pressfit setup" width="600"> <img src="pressex.png" alt="pressfit extrusion" width="600"> This is the lazer cut result of the pressfit puzzle.
